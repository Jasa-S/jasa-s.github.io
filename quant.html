# Ticker Sector Score Δ1d RSI Mom 12-1 RS 6m Trend

Methodology

Composite blends cross-sectional z-scores of 12-1 momentum (zM), volatility-adjusted return (zS), relative strength vs SPY (zRS), MACD histogram (zMACD), and normalized RSI (zRSI), plus a binary trend filter (price > SMA200 AND SMA50 > SMA200).

Score = 50 + 12.5 · clip(R, -4, 4), where R = 0.30·zM + 0.25·zS + 0.20·trend + 0.15·zRS + 0.05·zMACD + 0.05·zRSI. Verdict: BUY ≥ 70, WATCH 40-69, AVOID < 40. BUY downgrades to WATCH if the market regime is RISK_OFF or earnings are within 3 trading days. A separate PULLBACK overlay flags names in an uptrend pulling back to SMA50 with RSI < 35.

Holding actions: ADD (score ≥ 65, trend on, regime on, no imminent earnings); TRIM (score < 40, RSI > 80 with a large gain, or position oversized); HOLD otherwise. ADD sizing risks 1 % of capital per trade using a 2-ATR stop.

Not financial advice. Past performance is not indicative of future returns. This is a screener, not a backtest. Verify everything yourself.
